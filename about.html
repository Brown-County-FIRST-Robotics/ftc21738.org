About FTC

FIRST Tech Challenge students work together with their mentors to design and build dynamic robots to compete in an exciting game released every September. On and ff the field, students develop STEM skills, engage in community outreach, practice engineering innovation, and build confidence to help them succeed.

For more info go to https://browncountyfirstrobotics.org
